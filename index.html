Adobo Recipe

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 3-4 bay leaves
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions:

  • Heat the oil in a pan and saut√© the garlic and onion until fragrant.
  • Add the chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  • Add the vinegar, soy sauce, peppercorns, and bay leaves. Stir well.
  • Bring to a boil and then lower the heat. Cover and simmer for 30-45 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  • Serve hot with rice.
